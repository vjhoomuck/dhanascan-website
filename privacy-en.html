Privacy Policy

Privacy

DhanaScan is designed to provide a simple, reassuring and child-friendly educational experience.

No ads

DhanaScan does not contain advertising for children.

No personal data collection

The app does not collect children’s personal data.

No child account

No child account is required to use the main features.

Main features work offline

The main DhanaScan features can be used without an internet connection.

Camera

The camera is used only to scan DhanaScan educational cards.

No image sent to a server

Scanned images are not sent to a server.

Resources and content

Paid resources and YouTube episodes should be used with parental permission and supervision.
