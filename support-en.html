DhanaScan Support

Simple answers to use DhanaScan with cards, booklets and sounds.

How to use DhanaScan?

Print the Dhana cards, open the app, scan a card, then let the child listen and repeat the sound.

How to print cards and booklets?

Resources are available in the app and can be printed for personal family or educational use.

Sound issue

Check the device volume, silent mode and audio settings.

Camera issue

Make sure camera permission is enabled for DhanaScan in the device settings.

Download DhanaScan

The app is available on iOS and Android.
